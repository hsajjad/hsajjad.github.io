Hassan Sajjad

Associate Professor

Faculty of Computer Science, Dalhousie University, Halifax, Canada

I am a Faculty member in the Faculty of Computer Science and Director of HyperMatrix at Dalhousie University, Halifax, Canada. I am an AI researcher with domain expertise in Natural Language Processing and explainable AI. Moreover, I am a consultant and a mentor blended with entrepreneurial interests.

Research Interest

NLP, Deep learning, Trustworthy AI – language generation, interpretation of AI models, generalization and robustness, transfer learning
